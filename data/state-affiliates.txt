Causey — US Chess state affiliate websites
==========================================
Compiled for scraping / pathway coverage. Every USCF state affiliate sends
representatives to Denker, Barber, Rockefeller, Haring, and related national
invitationals — so these calendars matter even when volume is low.

Verify URLs before wiring a scraper; affiliates redesign calendars often.
Shared hubs are noted inline.
